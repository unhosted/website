Software evolution

Let me tell you about how software evolved, from desktop to the web, what the problems are and what we see as solution.

  • Basics of software: application and data

  • The data is your personal documents, photos, etc.

  • And there are many applications.

  • Secure but also confined to your personal computer.

  • That was the desktop. Today there are 3 big differences:

  • 1. Hosted software is an app+storage package deal.

  • 2. It's physically elsewhere, out of your control.

  • … mostly in California.

  • 3. But it's accessible from anywhere, with any internet-enabled device.

  • … your laptop …

  • … your phone …

  • … everything with a web browser.

A better architecture

People should have the versatility of the web and the safety of having their data in one place.

  • From the current architecture …

  • User data needs to be separated from the applications.

  • Then every time you use an application …

  • … app and data only meet in the browser.

  • No multiple accounts, no data lock-in – but data safety and full advantage of the web.
